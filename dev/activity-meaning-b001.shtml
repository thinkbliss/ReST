Instructions

Engage in 5 acts of kindness. These may be random (e.g. paying an expired parking meter) or non-random (e.g. paying a friend a stellar compliment) To get the most impact from this exercise, perform all 5 acts on one day, and vary what you do, i.e. do different things rather than the same thing 5 times.
