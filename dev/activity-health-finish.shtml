Tip!

Get the Right Light

To remedy sleep woes, try to get some morning exposure to natural daylight, or strong light with blue tones. In the evening, shift to softer light with red and orange tones, and limit exposure to bright lights, including electronic screens, as you approach sleep.3

Bottom Line: To brighten your mood, brighten your morning (and de-light your night).
