Tip!

Be the Boss … of your actions

We often let our emotions dictate what we do, waiting until we are confident or “in the right mood” before taking action. However, leading a fulfilling life requires that we repeatedly choose to do what is important to us, even if we feel like crap and would rather hide under the covers.

Bottom Line: Instead of trying to control your mood, control your actions, and your mood will eventually follow.
