Tip!

Take a hike!

Did you know that walking in the woods for as little as 15 minutes causes measurable physiological changes? A study in Japan found that a 15-minute nature walk led to a 16% decrease in the stress hormone cortisol, a 2% drop in blood pressure, and a 4% drop in heart rate.

Bottom Line: Go outside!
